Hey there! I'm Gabriel a designer based in the Philippines.

I am a graduate of De La Salle College Saint Benilde (DLS-CSB) and I studied Multimedia Arts (MMA), My Design style is minimalistic and focuses more on contrast and texture and making the designs stand out thorugh using space. I believe that there is a story in every design be it big or small there will always be a story.

"Sandbox" represents more than just a showcase; it's a journey back to the carefree days of our childhood, where the sandbox was our canvas for boundless imagination. This theme invites us to rediscover the pure joy of creativity, where every grain of sand holds the potential for something extraordinary. Just as we once sculpted castles and forged adventures in the sandbox, this event celebrates the transformative power of imagination. Through the top capstones of De La Salle College of Saint Benilde, "Sandbox" becomes a vibrant tapestry of innovation, reminding us that the most profound ideas often originate from moments of playful exploration.
